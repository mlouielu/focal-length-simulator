Motivation

Numerous time, there is such a moment as a hobby photographer that I take the bird in flight photo when I am using a standard focal length lens.

I always wonder the same question after I took that shoot: What is the exact focal length that I need to get that shoot perfectly - or at least acceptable?

There are a lot of focal length simulators on the Internet, but none of them support to use your own photo.

So I decided to build one, and this is it! The only focal length simulator that support to use your own photos!

How To Use

Basic

The red bounding box indicate the photo size of the current focal length, it should be the same at the beginning.

You can drag, zoom in, and zoom out to interact and simulate different focal length with the photo.

Use Your Own Photo

It is totally offline! Local! Nothing will upload to any cloud or server.

Click "Choose File" and select the photo you want to simulate on, and the photo will be replace to the one you selected.

This tool will try to get the lens focal length from EXIF in your photo, you will need to manually input it if it failed.

After loading your photo, click "Reset Zoom" to reset the red bounding box

How To Zoom In

There are five ways to zoom in:

  1. Double Tap
  2. Gesture Zoom
  3. Wheel Scrolling
  4. Range Bar
  5. Manual Enter
